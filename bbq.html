Sheet metal bbq

The task presented was centered around the creation of a sheet metal barbecue design. The primary objective was to gain proficiency in the manipulation of sheet metal, understanding its behavior and folding intricacies, alongside proficiency in utilizing the Siemens NX software. The accompanying imagery comprises renders meticulously crafted utilizing Autodesk VRED, a decision influenced by the constraints of the ongoing COVID-19 situation during the course of the assignment.
