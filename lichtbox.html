Lightbox

Create your own world. It is made out of lasercut en 3d-printed parts. The Lightbox uses a ATtiny85 to operate.

There are 3 PCBs avaible and 2 types of test boards. a PCB for programming the ATtiny, one for testing the ATtiny and one to use in the final product.
The test boards make it easy to test and play around with the color combinations.

The participants of the camp learn how to make and program a Lightbox.
