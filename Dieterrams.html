Retro Game

A trowback to the old gaming consoles. This one you can make at home. it s made out of lasercut en 3d-printed parts. The hart of the console is a small Arduino Nano.

The larger boards can be used at a camp where young people learn to program. The program can be easily tested on the larger pre-made boards. later the they build the smaller version and upload the program.

The participants of the camp learn how to make and program a game computer.
